For this assignment we had to make an Encyclopedia Vulgaris of at least 100 objects of choice. I chose to collect stuff that I could find in my dorm. I photographed or scanned them and divided them into three categories: rectangles, cirkels and triangles.

For this assignment we had to make an Encyclopedia Vulgaris of at least 100 objects of choice. I chose to collect stuff that I could find in my dorm. I photographed or scanned them and divided them into three categories: rectangles, cirkels and triangles.

These are three EP's I designed. The teachers gave us two EP's, 'Songs of British Birds' and 'Oblique Strategies'(which is actually not an EP but a box full of cards, which you should read in case you have a creative block, made by Brian Eno), and we had to choose one extra EP. For this assignment we also had to make a font which fits the design and theme of your design. I found a card in Oblique Strategies that said 'Go outside, shut the door' and the bird songs 'Songs of British Birds' brings us immediatly to nature. So my third EP is 'What a Wonderful World' by Louis Armstrong. For the font I chose to make the letters as sound frequencies, based on the sonograms of birds singing.

This poster is made for Woody Allen's movie
'Bananas'. We were asked to choose one of the 8 given directors and design three posters for three different movies of that director. The posters had to be an identity for the director. I cut the faces in half en put them on the edges of the poster which should give you a neurotic feeling. Woody Allen was very neurotic.
